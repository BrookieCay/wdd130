Join our high adventure multi-day whitewater trips offering guides, delicious meals, and all camping and boating equiptment needed.

Along with the delicious food and fun, all our trips offer whitewater rafting training and excllent qualified gudes. Our expeditions range from half-day to five-day trips covering distances from twelve to eighty eight miles of exciting whitewater expeditions for all to enjoy. We provide all meals, snacks, and rest stops needed. If on sleep in comfort on our provided quality bedding and tents. Call ahead and we can help arrange transportation needs for you and your group.

We offer exciting expeditions for families. We custom make family and group expeditions to fit that group's needs. We do limit age with children to four years and older for only whitewater levels one and two. Our six and over expeditions range from level two to three. Twelve and over may do level three. High adventures go through the three through five level range. We offer on our "Explore" trips hiking, fishing, and geology exploration. Bring your family, youth groups, or high adventures and find your exciting expedition. Call us and we will help you fit your needs to the adventure and exciting expedition you wish to find.
