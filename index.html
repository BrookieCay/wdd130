Brookie Cay Swallow | WDD 130

I am a retired school teacher and mother of many children. I want to learn how to make a website to go with my Family History classes. I graduated from B.Y.U. and taught school in Idaho. I married and our family was in the Airforce. I have lived in Idaho, Nevada, Utah, Idaho, Maryland, Alabama, Mississippi, Texas, Oklahoma, New Mexico, Iowa and Nevada again. My children are all grown up, and I visit them all I can. I love outdoors and adventure. In Nevada I can show you hidden caves and fossils. I love working with children and helping them find the world that they love. I play the guitar and fun piano songs that help make the world a fun place to be. My Family History Classes have taught me the adventure of finding hidden information about the lives of my ancestors and others I research. I try to remember to write history now with living and doing things with my family. I have done stained glass art work, writing, and love to travel to see my family. I am currently helping tend a 5 month old grandson each day and learning to make this website.

Nevada

Nevada is a beautiful mountanious western desert state in the United States. It is bordered by California on the West, Oregon and Idaho in the North, and Utah and Arizona on the East. It includes the cities of Reno the state capital, and Las Vegas, the world's gambling capitol. In between are many small rural farming and mining communities.
